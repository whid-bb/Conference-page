New Technologies Conference

The relentless pace of technology change gives applications and software engineering leaders a major opportunity to not simply respond to change, but to lead it. By architecting, developing and modernizing systems to utilize emerging technologies, organizations can deliver solutions for improved operations, increased agility and better business outcomes. The result will be a strengthened foundation of trust in the solutions delivered by the organization.

From this foundation of trust, comes the confidence to build. To master the ability to create, buy and modify solutions that not only meet user and customer needs, but also predict and steer those needs. Software itself can become a catalyst for change: business change, cultural change – even world change. Rather than a liability or sunk cost, applications can become a source of innovation and competitive advantage.
